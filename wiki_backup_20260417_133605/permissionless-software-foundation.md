# Permissionless Software Foundation (PSF)

**Summary**: The organization that maintains the [Cash Stack](cash-stack-layers.md) and works to develop software for privacy, censorship resistance, and economic freedom.

**Sources**: intro.md

**Last updated**: 2026-04-17

---

The Permissionless Software Foundation (PSF) maintains the [Cash Stack](cash-stack-layers.md) (source: intro.md). Its primary mission is to "develop, promote, and maintain software that makes it easy for individuals to protect their privacy, circumvent censorship, and engage in economic activity" (source: intro.md).
