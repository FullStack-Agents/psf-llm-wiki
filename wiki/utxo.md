# UTXO

**Summary**: Unspent Transaction Output; the fundamental unit of currency in Bitcoin Cash.

**Sources**: mastering-bitcoin-cash_transactions_4.md

**Last updated**: 2026-04-17

---

A UTXO (Unspent Transaction Output) is an indivisible chunk of currency locked to a specific owner and recognized by the network (source: mastering-bitcoin-cash_transactions_4.md).

### Key Properties
- **No Accounts**: There are no account balances in Bitcoin Cash; instead, balances are derived by summing all UTXOs belonging to a user (source: mastering-bitcoin-cash_transactions_4.md).
- **Denomination**: Values are measured in satoshis (1 BCH = 100,000,000 satoshis) (source: mastering-bitcoin-cash_transactions_4.md).
- **Spending**: UTXOs must be consumed in their entirety. Any excess value is returned to the sender as change (source: mastering-bitcoin-cash_transactions_4.md).
