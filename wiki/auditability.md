# Auditability

**Summary**: The ability to trace the history of funds on the Bitcoin Cash blockchain while maintaining user pseudonymity.

**Sources**: mastering-bitcoin-cash_chapter-2-how-bicoin-cash-works_11.md

**Last updated**: 2026-04-17

---

Because of the double-entry bookkeeping model, the Bitcoin Cash ledger is inherently auditable.

### Transparency vs. Pseudonymity
- **Transparency**: The flow of funds can be traced back through the entire transaction history to the original point where the coins were mined (the [coinbase-transaction](coinbase-transaction.md)).
- **Pseudonymity**: Users are identified by cryptographic addresses rather than personal identities, allowing for privacy while the ledger remains open for audit.
