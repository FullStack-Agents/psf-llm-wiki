# Bitcoin Cash Fundamentals Summary

**Summary**: An overview of Bitcoin Cash (BCH) as a peer-to-peer electronic cash system for decentralized digital value transfer.

**Sources**: mastering-bitcoin-cash_chapter-1-What-is-Bitcoin-Cash-(BCH)_1.md

**Last updated**: 2026-04-16

---

Bitcoin Cash (BCH) is a decentralized peer-to-peer electronic cash system that allows for the transfer of digital value across the Internet (source: mastering-bitcoin-cash_chapter-1-What-is-Bitcoin-Cash-(BCH)_1.md). It functions similarly to conventional currencies, facilitating the buying and selling of goods and extending credit.

The system is implemented via open-source software and is accessible across various devices, including smartphones and laptops. Ownership of BCH is managed through cryptographic keys stored in [[digital-wallets]], where the possession of a private key is the sole requirement for spending funds (source: mastering-bitcoin-cash_chapter-1-What-is-Bitcoin-Cash-(BCH)_1.md).
