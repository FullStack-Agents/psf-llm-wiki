# Mining

**Summary**: The process of verifying transactions and adding them to the blockchain.

**Sources**: mastering-bitcoin-cash_transactions_1.md

**Last updated**: 2026-04-17

---

Mining nodes are responsible for verifying transactions and including them in blocks (source: mastering-bitcoin-cash_transactions_1.md). Once a block is mined and confirmed by subsequent blocks, the transaction becomes permanent.
