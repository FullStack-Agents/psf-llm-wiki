# Monetary Policy

**Summary**: The predetermined rules governing the creation and total supply of Bitcoin Cash.

**Sources**: mastering-bitcoin-cash_chapter-1-What-is-Bitcoin-Cash-(BCH)_2.md

**Last updated**: 2026-04-16

---

The monetary policy of Bitcoin Cash is designed to be inherently deflationary by preventing arbitrary inflation (source: mastering-bitcoin-cash_chapter-1-What-is-Bitcoin-Cash-(BCH)_2.md).

### Key Constraints
- **Supply Cap**: The total supply is capped at 21 million coins, which is projected to be reached by the year 2140 (source: mastering-bitcoin-cash_chapter-1-What-is-Bitcoin-Cash-(BCH)_2.md).
- **Halving**: The rate of new coin creation is halved every four years (source: mastering-bitcoin-cash_chapter-1-What-is-Bitcoin-Cash-(BCH)_2.md).
