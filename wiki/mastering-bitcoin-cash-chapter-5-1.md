# Mastering Bitcoin Cash Chapter 5 Part 1

**Summary**: Introduction to the Bitcoin Cash P2P network architecture, describing its flat topology and the concept of the "extended Bitcoin Cash network."

**Sources**: mastering-bitcoin-cash_chapter-5-the-bitcoin-cash-network_1.md

**Last updated**: 2026-04-17

---

Bitcoin Cash utilizes a peer-to-peer (P2P) network architecture where all computers are equal peers. This flat topology ensures the network is resilient, decentralized, and open, which is critical for achieving decentralized control (source: mastering-bitcoin-cash_chapter-5-the-bitcoin-cash-network_1.md).

The network is categorized into two layers:
1. **Bitcoin Cash P2P Network**: Nodes running the core Bitcoin Cash P2P protocol.
2. **Extended Bitcoin Cash Network**: This includes the P2P network plus additional protocols like Stratum (used for mining and lightweight wallets). Gateway routing servers bridge these different protocols together (source: mastering-bitcoin-cash_chapter-5-the-bitcoin-cash-network_1.md).
