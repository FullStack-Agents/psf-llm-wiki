# Double-Spending

**Summary**: The challenge of ensuring that a digital asset cannot be spent more than once.

**Sources**: mastering-bitcoin-cash_chapter-1-What-is-Bitcoin-Cash-(BCH)_3.md

**Last updated**: 2026-04-16

---

Double-spending is one of the two fundamental challenges of digital money, alongside authenticity (source: mastering-bitcoin-cash_chapter-1-What-is-Bitcoin-Cash-(BCH)_3.md). Because digital data can be copied perfectly, a system must ensure that the same digital unit of currency cannot be spent twice.

In traditional centralized systems, this is prevented by a central clearinghouse that maintains the account balances. Bitcoin Cash solves this in a decentralized manner using a public ledger ([[blockchain]]).
