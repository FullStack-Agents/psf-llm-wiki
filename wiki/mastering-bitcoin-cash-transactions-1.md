## Transaction Lifecycle

Transactions are the core of the [bitcoin-cash](bitcoin-cash.md) system, serving as a public entry in the [blockchain](blockchain.md)'s global double-entry bookkeeping ledger (source: mastering-bitcoin-cash_transactions_1.md).

The lifecycle of a transaction consists of several stages:
1. **Origination**: The transaction is created.
2. **Signing**: One or more digital signatures authorize the spending of funds (source: mastering-bitcoin-cash_transactions_1.md).
3. **Broadcasting**: The transaction is sent to the [bitcoin-cash](bitcoin-cash.md) network, where nodes validate and propagate it (source: mastering-bitcoin-cash_transactions_1.md).
4. **Mining**: A mining node verifies the transaction and includes it in a block (source: mastering-bitcoin-cash_transactions_1.md).
5. **Confirmation**: Once recorded and confirmed by subsequent blocks, the transaction is permanent, and the funds are available for future spending (source: mastering-bitcoin-cash_transactions_1.md).
