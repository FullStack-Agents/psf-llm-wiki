# Decentralization

**Summary**: The architectural principle of operating a system without central servers or controlling authorities.

**Sources**: mastering-bitcoin-cash_chapter-1-What-is-Bitcoin-Cash-(BCH)_2.md

**Last updated**: 2026-04-16

---

Bitcoin Cash is built as a distributed, peer-to-peer (P2P) system. This means it functions without any central server or single point of control (source: mastering-bitcoin-cash_chapter-1-What-is-Bitcoin-Cash-(BCH)_2.md). 

Unlike early digital currencies of the late 1980s, which were centralized and thus vulnerable to government intervention and hacking attacks, Bitcoin Cash uses distributed computation to eliminate single points of failure (source: mastering-bitcoin-cash_chapter-1-What-is-Bitcoin-Cash-(BCH)_3.md).

This architecture allows any network participant to contribute as a miner, utilizing their computing power to maintain the integrity of the blockchain.
