# Transaction Broadcasting

**Summary**: The process of transmitting a signed transaction to the Bitcoin Cash network.

**Sources**: mastering-bitcoin-cash_transactions_2.md

**Last updated**: 2026-04-17

---

Broadcasting requires delivering a transaction to any Bitcoin Cash node to ensure it is propagated and eventually included in the blockchain (source: mastering-bitcoin-cash_transactions_2.md).

Transactions can be transmitted over any secure or insecure network because they are digitally signed and contain no confidential information (source: mastering-bitcoin-cash_transactions_2.md). Examples of transmission methods include:
- WiFi, Bluetooth, and NFC
- Text messages
- Forum posts (source: mastering-bitcoin-cash_transactions_2.md)
