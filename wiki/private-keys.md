# Private Keys

**Summary**: Cryptographic keys used to authorize transactions and prove ownership of Bitcoin Cash.

**Sources**: mastering-bitcoin-cash_chapter-1-What-is-Bitcoin-Cash-(BCH)_1.md

**Last updated**: 2026-04-16

---

A private key is the essential cryptographic requirement to unlock and spend bitcoins (source: mastering-bitcoin-cash_chapter-1-What-is-Bitcoin-Cash-(BCH)_1.md). Possession of the private key is the sole requirement for spending funds.

Private keys are typically stored within [[digital-wallets]] to keep them secure. If a private key is lost, the funds associated with it become inaccessible.
