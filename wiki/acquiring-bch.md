# Acquiring [Bitcoin Cash](bitcoin-cash.md)

**Summary**: The various methods available for new users to obtain [Bitcoin Cash](bitcoin-cash.md), ranging from free faucets to peer-to-peer exchanges.

---

There are several ways for individuals to acquire their first [Bitcoin Cash](bitcoin-cash.md), each with different trade-offs regarding convenience, privacy, and accessibility (source: mastering-bitcoin-cash_chapter-1-What-is-Bitcoin-Cash-([BCH](bitcoin-cash.md))_8.md).

### Methods of Acquisition
- **Free Faucets**: Some services, such as free.bitcoin.com, provide small amounts of [BCH](bitcoin-cash.md) to new users upon registration.
- **Cryptocurrency Exchanges**: Centralized platforms (e.g., Coinbase) allow users to purchase [BCH](bitcoin-cash.md) using traditional fiat currency.
- **Peer-to-Peer (P2P) Exchange**: Direct purchases from friends or acquaintances who already own [BCH](bitcoin-cash.md).
- **Local Marketplaces**: Services like localbitcoincash.org facilitate in-person cash transactions between local buyers and sellers.
- **Accepting Payment**: Earning [BCH](bitcoin-cash.md) by selling products or professional services (e.g., programming).
- **[Bitcoin Cash](bitcoin-cash.md) ATMs**: Physical kiosks that allow for the purchase of [BCH](bitcoin-cash.md) using cash.
