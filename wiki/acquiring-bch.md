# Acquiring Bitcoin Cash

**Summary**: The various methods available for new users to obtain Bitcoin Cash, ranging from free faucets to peer-to-peer exchanges.

**Sources**: mastering-bitcoin-cash_chapter-1-What-is-Bitcoin-Cash-(BCH)_8.md

**Last updated**: 2026-04-16

---

There are several ways for individuals to acquire their first Bitcoin Cash, each with different trade-offs regarding convenience, privacy, and accessibility (source: mastering-bitcoin-cash_chapter-1-What-is-Bitcoin-Cash-(BCH)_8.md).

### Methods of Acquisition
- **Free Faucets**: Some services, such as free.bitcoin.com, provide small amounts of BCH to new users upon registration.
- **Cryptocurrency Exchanges**: Centralized platforms (e.g., Coinbase) allow users to purchase BCH using traditional fiat currency.
- **Peer-to-Peer (P2P) Exchange**: Direct purchases from friends or acquaintances who already own BCH.
- **Local Marketplaces**: Services like localbitcoincash.org facilitate in-person cash transactions between local buyers and sellers.
- **Accepting Payment**: Earning BCH by selling products or professional services (e.g., programming).
- **Bitcoin Cash ATMs**: Physical kiosks that allow for the purchase of BCH using cash.
